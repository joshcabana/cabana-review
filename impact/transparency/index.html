How we calculate 10%

10% is calculated on merchandise subtotal after discounts, excluding shipping.

Allocation (Men/Women)

Designated proportion varies by collection focus. Men’s purchases prioritise men’s mental health; women’s purchases prioritise women’s empowerment.
